# Search Strategies by Sector

## Technology
- Search: "{company} quarterly earnings {year}"
- Search: "{company} product launch announcement"
- Search: "{company} AI strategy"
- Focus on: revenue growth, R&D spending, product pipeline

## Automotive / Energy
- Search: "{company} delivery numbers quarterly"
- Search: "{company} production capacity"
- Search: "{company} regulatory approval"
- Focus on: unit deliveries, margins, energy revenue mix

## Semiconductors
- Search: "{company} data center revenue"
- Search: "{company} chip export controls"
- Search: "{company} GPU announcement"
- Focus on: data center vs gaming split, gross margins, supply constraints

## Financial Services
- Search: "{company} quarterly earnings net interest"
- Search: "{company} loan loss provisions"
- Focus on: NII, credit quality, capital ratios

## General (fallback)
- Search: "{company} latest news {month} {year}"
- Search: "{company} financial results"
- Search: "{company} SEC filing 10-Q"
- Focus on: revenue, profit, notable events
